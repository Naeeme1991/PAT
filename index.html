What We Do

Pishrovane Arse Tejarat (PAT) is a commercial and technical company

Pishrovane Arse Tejarat (PAT) is a commercial and technical company which has been established mainly for export and import purposes as well as other subjects(see: “About PAT”) that registered on October 24th, 2005.

PAT is located in Tehran and managers of this company aim to develop / re-establish business-related activities after the sanctions are lifted.

  • About PAT

    PAT is authorized

    To do all commercial affairs including purchasing, selling , distributing , importing and exporting of all permitted goods and products; To issue license for local clients as local agents; To sell products / materials as an official-licensed agent / representative of foreign as well as inland companies;

  • Further Tasks

    PAT intends

    To clear the imported goods from the Iranian customs after signing the contracts with the importers; To sign contracts in order to design, supervise, install, renew, repair and provide services for machineries of manufactories and factories;

  • More Services

    PAT aims

    To provide technical and consulting services for civil engineering contractors / construction engineering companies; To produce all kinds of products including raw materials, spare parts, etc.

International Conference on Industrial Chimneys and Cooling Towers ICCT 2016 Rotterdam, NL, October 05-08, 2016 (ICCT2016)

ICCT 2016, the International Conference on Industrial Chimneys and Cooling Towers will take place in Rotterdam, Netherlands, October 5 - 8, 2016. It follows the successful ICCT 2014 in Prague in 2014, which has been attended by more than 200 experts from all over the world. MSc, PhD in structural engineering Omid Pouran  is a research and teaching assistant at the Institute of Statics and Dynamics of Structures (at Wuppertal University, Germany and member of Kraetzig & Partner Engineering Co, Bochum, Germany).  Mr. O. Pouran has been active in ICCT 2014 and the member of organizing committee who presented an article titled “Interaction and Intersection in Research and Development of Cooling Towers and Concrete Chimneys”. He is also a member of organizing committee at ICCT 2016. PAT will be his third sponsor in this event.
